History

TechCon is a conference focused on technical aspects of radio and audio industries. It evolved from being part of the UK Radio Festival, organized by The Radio Academy, to being independently run by TBC Media Ltd. since 2016. TBC Media Ltd. was formed by former TechCon committee members, continuing the conference's focus on supporting the technical community.




Mission

TechCon 2024 aimed to be a premier event for exploring the latest technological advancements and fostering collaboration within the tech community. Its goals included educating and informing attendees, facilitating networking opportunities, driving innovation, and showcasing excellence in various tech fields. The conference served as a platform for industry leaders, innovators, and professionals to connect, share ideas, and explore the future of technology.




Past Speakers

Andrew Pearce

A computer graphics pioneer and patent producer, Pearce is known for his work at DreamWorks Animation, where he directed the R&D team. He is also a board member of the Academy Software Foundation, and his work highlights the importance of interdisciplinary interests and well-rounded education.




Alex Sheen

As the founder of "Because I Said I Would," Sheen advocates for accountability and commitment, particularly in how technology impacts human connection and customer experience. His work emphasizes the need to balance technological advancement with human values.




Jason Rostar

Rostar leads the firm-wide strategic implementation of technology at HED. His expertise lies in leveraging innovative software and data-driven decision-making to enhance architectural and engineering practices.




Annie

As the Director of Product Inclusion & Equity at Google, Annie leads initiatives to ensure equitable product design for all users, focusing on historically marginalized groups. She also consults with various Alphabet companies on product inclusion and equity strategies.
